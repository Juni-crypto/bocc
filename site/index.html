What's inside

Google-Photos intelligence, built for one night

Selfie find-my-photos

One selfie, every photo you're in. Face clustering runs across the whole pooled gallery, so nobody scrolls 400 shots looking for themselves.

Search in plain words

"People near the cake." "Golden hour." OCR and CLIP search, no tags needed.

Venue geofencing

Only accept photos shot at the venue. Keep the gallery on-site and on-theme.

Per-guest caps

Five, fifteen, or unlimited. A cap forces curation, so every shot earns its place.

Auto-highlights

The sharpest, best-lit frames pulled into a recap reel automatically.

Map & moments

Photos pinned where they were shot, auto-grouped into ceremony, dinner, afterparty.

Consent, by design

Face matching that asks first

Faces are biometric data. Consent is part of the join flow, not buried in a policy, so every guest opts in before a single face is processed. GDPR and BIPA grade, from day one.

For hosts

Run the night, not the upload queue

  • Create from web or phone, full control over every setting
  • Live stats: crew joined, photos pooled, faces found
  • Moderation queue, expiry, and one-tap export of the album

For guests

Scan, shoot, find yourself

  • Join in one scan, no app store, no password
  • See the whole night from every other guest's angle
  • One selfie, then download every photo you're in

Under the hood

Self-hosted engine. Your photos stay yours.

The AI runs on a self-hosted Immich engine behind our own backend, pointed at storage you control. Face detection, semantic search, and OCR happen on infrastructure you can hold, not a black box you rent.
